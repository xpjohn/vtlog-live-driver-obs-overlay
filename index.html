VTLOG Live Driver OBS Overlay v1.0.0

A 1024×75 Browser Source for OBS Studio that shows your live VTLOG job data.

1. Configure

  1. Enter your VTLOG profile link:
    Enter a valid VTLOG profile URL or Steam ID64.
  2. Enter your VTLOG VTC link or ID:
    Enter a VTC ID (e.g. 5812) or a vtlog.net/vtc/… URL.
  3. Enter your VTC logo image URL (optional):
    Any direct image URL. Leave blank to hide the VTC logo.
    Transparent logos / favicons with negative space are only recommended for light mode.
  4. Pick a background color:
    #121212
  5. Set background opacity:
    0.8
  6. Copy the generated CSS:

2. Add to OBS

  1. In your scene, click + in the Sources panel and select Browser.
  2. Give it a name (e.g. VTLOG Live) and click OK.
  3. Set URL to:
    Enter your VTC link or ID above.
  4. Set Width to 1024 and Height to 75.
  5. Scroll down to Custom CSS, clear the default content, and paste the copied CSS.
  6. Click OK, then position and resize the source in your scene as needed.
  7. Right-click the source → Interact, then log in to VTLOG with your Steam account. OBS keeps the session active between restarts. Refresh the source to update the overlay.
Session expired? If the overlay shows a login button instead of job data, right-click the source → Interact and sign in again. Refresh the source to update the overlay.

Usage

  • Start a job in-game. Your job appears on the VTC live page and the overlay fades in automatically.
  • The overlay fades out when the job ends or you go offline.
  • Regenerate and repaste the CSS if you change your Steam ID, logo, or colors.

Requirements

  • OBS Studio 28+ (ships with a built-in Chromium browser source).
  • A VTLOG account linked to your Steam profile.
  • Your account must be visible on the live drivers page in your VTC.
